About ScoreCarts

ScoreCarts is a real-time retail and brand research solution with the aim of improving the relationship between brands and their products on shelves and acquiring the right tools for their businesses. We built ScoreCarts with two goals in mind, one being servicing the brands and providing them with deeper insights and analysis about their products/markets/shoppers, and the second is to enable companies and businesses to create and build their own digital customized tools and dashboards. Our mission is to close the gap between brands and the right insights and take part in their journey to leadership.
