OUR SERVICES

At the core of ScoreCarts is you, the Brand, and our main focus is to create and develop tailor-made solutions to be able to answer all of your business’s needs and requirements. Our solutions include.

Our main focus

# 1
Service

Retail Live Market Tracking:

Through our Agents in the field, Brands are able to log in daily to ScoreCarts’ portal and view their brand’s performance in real-time. The dashboard section has a summary of the overall brand health trackers, and the analysis section has a more dynamic outlook where brands are able to filter through the data, view various charts and reports, export data, and create shortcuts for their search inquiries. The portal also features a live Interactive Map which allows you to see pictures of each retailer on a live map.

# 2
Service

Retail Shopper Insights:

Brands are able to get accurate and direct insights right from the shoppers by asking them any questions or reviews for their products. Through our Shopper App, getting in touch with the consumers is now just a simple click of a button, literally, where Brands get to choose the profile of the shoppers and have them participate in a survey. You may also recruit them to become your mystery shoppers and conduct field visits.

# 3
Service

Customized Dashboards:

We create and develop customized digital platforms and tools which allow Brands to transform their data into an online and interactive dashboard, easy to use and filter through the data. Functionalities and look and feel are all built from scratch to ensure that each dashboard is uniquely crafted for each business.

# 4
Service

AI Image Recognition:

Brands are now able to process and analyze all types of images in a matter of seconds using our AI Image Recognition technology. Entering the era of digital data, we have built Bella with the aim of generating faster and more accurate big data computing and revolutionize the way research and analysis are done.
