Ask Bella

Bella

How do you know what products look like?

Every day, I’m fed thousands of images, videos & 3d models of hundreds of products by the ScoreCarts team. I use deep learning datasets to identify patterns in the images. I process each image & each frame individually, in different brightness, contrast & saturation levels. Which enables me to recognize almost instantaneously, & list it in its specific place in my database.

Bella

How many objects can you identify at the same time?

It has been a long run, I started with 3 objects per image. And today I am able to identify up to 428 objects per image and still improving every day.
