Meet Bella

Meet Bella, Bella is an AI image recognition algorithm that achieves above human-level performance and real-time object detection. she analyzes and processes thousands of images within seconds and is able to identify objects using image recognition with an accuracy of 94%. Bella is a pioneer in the retail field as it is fed hundreds of thousands of images a day, and each shelf image contains up to 350 product faces. This means millions of products are analyzed by Bella each day to detect which products are missing, how many faces are present per product, brand & model. This gives brands accurate, real-time reports on their presence in retail spaces, and that gives an advantage over their competitors by being 3 steps ahead by knowing the market’s reality. And in today’s world, the largest market share belongs to whoever gets intel first. And thanks to Bella, that can be you.
